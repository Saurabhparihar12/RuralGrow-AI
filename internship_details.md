# AI Internship Project Plan & Roadmap

## Overview
- **Selected Role:** AI-Assisted Full Stack Development
- **Choice 1 (Primary):** Homestay & Eco-tourism (Project: **AI-Powered Homestay Guest Review Sentiment Classifier**)
- **Choice 2 (Secondary):** Food Processing (Project: **AI-Powered Rural Business Growth Assistant**)

We are proceeding with a consolidated custom project: **AI-Powered Rural Business Growth Assistant**, which integrates the core features of the Homestay Review Sentiment Classifier but scales it to multiple rural sectors (Agriculture, Food Processing, Handloom, Herbal, and Homestay Packages).

---

## Detailed Project Descriptions

### 1. AI-Powered Homestay Guest Review Sentiment Classifier
- **Sector:** Homestay & Eco-tourism
- **Goal:** Automate analysis of guest reviews for homestays to help owners understand guest feedback and reply professionally.
- **Workflow:**
  1. User pastes review.
  2. AI detects sentiment (Positive/Neutral/Negative).
  3. AI classifies theme (Food, Cleanliness, Location, etc.).
  4. AI generates professional suggested reply.
- **Tech Stack:** React (Frontend), Node.js/Express (Backend), Gemini API (AI), MongoDB (Database).

### 2. AI-Powered Rural Business Growth Assistant (Consolidated Business Assistant)
- **Sector:** Food Processing, Agriculture, Handloom, Herbal, Homestay Packages
- **Goal:** A broader AI tool for rural enterprises to assist with marketing content, customer review analysis, and analytics.
- **Features:**
  1. **Review Analyzer:** Analyzes guest reviews, detects sentiment, classifies themes, and suggests replies.
  2. **Social Media Post Generator:** Generates Instagram/Facebook posts based on product details/prices.
  3. **WhatsApp Promotion Generator:** Generates marketing copy for WhatsApp campaigns.
  4. **Dashboard:** Displays statistics/analytics of reviews and marketing performance.

---

## System Details & Architecture

### Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** React.js, Tailwind CSS (or Vanilla CSS)
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
- **Database:** MongoDB Atlas
- **AI Integration:** Gemini API
- **Deployment:** Vercel (Frontend), Render (Backend)

### Database Design & Collections
- `users`: User registration and authentication details.
- `products`: Details of products/services (name, sector, description, price).
- `reviews`: Customer reviews (review text, sentiment, theme, product reference).
- `generated_content`: Marketing copy generated by Gemini (type: WhatsApp/Social, prompt used, output, product reference).
- `analytics`: Performance metrics, review stats, and marketing engagement data.

### Sample Data Requirements (Recommended)
- **Products:** 50 sample products (10 per sector: Agriculture, Food Processing, Handloom, Herbal, Homestay Packages).
- **Reviews:** 100 sample reviews (40 Positive, 30 Neutral, 30 Negative).
- *Note: Gemini can be used to generate these sample datasets.*
